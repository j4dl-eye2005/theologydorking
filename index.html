Theologia Operatorum
A Compendium of Google Dorking Commands for the Theological Scholar

These search incantations, when spoken to the engine of Google, shall reveal
hidden manuscripts, sacred texts, and scholarly disputations.

Core Operators
filetype:
Restrict to file type
site:
Search one domain
intitle:
Word in page title
inurl:
Word in URL
intext:
Word in body text
"quotes"
Exact phrase match
-word
Exclude a term
OR
Either term

✦ Scribal Counsel

Combine operators freely: filetype:pdf site:.edu "pneumatology" -filetype:doc sharpens results considerably. For rare texts, try intext:"rare" OR "manuscript" alongside your terms. The OR operator must be capitalized. Parentheses group alternatives: ("term1" OR "term2"). Add a year range using after:2010 before:2023 for recent scholarship.

Seek and ye shall find · These commands open freely accessible documents only · Always cite thy sources
